---
title: Caching and fair use
description: Cache behavior, rate-limit policy, and good-citizen guidelines for API consumers.
sidebar:
  order: 3
---

- Responses are edge-cached and marked `max-age=3600` - the dataset only
  changes when a new patch is ingested, so cache freely and honor
  `Cache-Control`.
- There are no hard rate limits today. Be a good citizen: cache what you fetch,
  use `/v1/db` for bulk needs instead of crawling every class, and identify
  your tool with a `User-Agent`.
- Access controls (limits, keys) may be added operationally if needed; they do
  not restrict what you may do with content you have lawfully obtained.
